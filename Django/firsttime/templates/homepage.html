What are softskills ?

Soft skills, also known as "people skills" or "interpersonal skills," refer to a set of personal attributes and abilities that enable effective communication, collaboration, and interaction with others. Unlike technical or hard skills, which are specific to a particular job or industry, soft skills are transferable across various domains and are essential for success in both personal and professional contexts.
